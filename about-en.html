Professional Manufacturing, Excellent Quality - Leader in Cutting Tool Industry

Home / About Us

Leader in Cutting Tool Industry

Dalian Tianyi Cutting Tools Co., Ltd. is located in the coastal city of Dalian, surrounded by mountains and sea with convenient transportation. We specialize in the field of superhard tools, producing diamond (PCD) and cubic boron nitride (PCBN) tools. Since our establishment in 2009, we have developed a stable production scale. Our products are ISO9001 certified and have earned customer recognition for their reliable quality.

We possess advanced manufacturing equipment and a professional technical team, dedicated to providing customers with high-quality, high-precision tooling solutions. Our products are widely used in automotive manufacturing, aerospace, precision machinery, electronics, and many other industries.

"Quality first, never satisfied" is our eternal pursuit. We always adhere to a customer-centric approach, driven by technological innovation, and supported by excellent service, growing together with our customers to create brilliance.
