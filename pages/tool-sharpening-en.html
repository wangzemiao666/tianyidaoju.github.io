Sharpening Process

Standardized five-step sharpening process ensures each tool restores optimal performance

01

Tool Inspection & Evaluation

Comprehensive inspection using precision measuring equipment to assess wear, geometric accuracy, and repair feasibility, developing a customized sharpening plan.

02

Cleaning & Pre-treatment

Thorough removal of oil, chips, and contaminants using standard cleaning techniques, preparing for subsequent sharpening steps.

03

Precision Sharpening

Using high-end CNC grinding machines with diamond wheels, precise sharpening according to original design parameters to restore geometric accuracy.

04

Edge Treatment & Polishing

Precision processing and polishing of the sharpened edge to ensure cutting edge sharpness Ra≤0.2μm, improving cutting performance and tool life.

05

Final Inspection & Packaging

Full dimensional inspection after sharpening; qualified products undergo rust prevention treatment and professional packaging.
