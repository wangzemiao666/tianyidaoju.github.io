After-sales Service Process

Standardized service process ensures timely and effective handling of every customer issue

01

Issue Receipt & Registration

Customer submits service request via phone, WeChat, or email; customer service records issue details, contact information, and service needs.

02

Initial Diagnosis & Response

Technical support engineer contacts customer within 2 hours, understands issue details remotely, provides preliminary solution or guidance.

03

Solution Formulation & Confirmation

Based on complexity, formulate detailed solution including service method (remote/onsite), estimated time and cost, confirm with customer.

04

Service Implementation

Implement service according to confirmed plan: remote support immediately, on-site service dispatched as scheduled.

05

Problem Resolution & Acceptance

After service completion, verify problem resolution, customer accepts and confirms service result, fill in satisfaction evaluation.

06

Service Tracking & Follow-up

Follow up within 7 days after service completion, understand service effect, collect improvement suggestions, establish customer service file.
