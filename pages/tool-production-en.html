Production Process

From raw materials to finished products, we strictly execute every process to ensure superior tool performance

01

Material Selection & Inspection

All raw materials come from well-known domestic and international suppliers and undergo strict quality inspection before entering the factory. PCD/PCBN compacts are tested for hardness, density, and internal defects using high-precision instruments to ensure material performance meets standards.

02

Design Optimization & Programming

Based on customer requirements and workpiece material characteristics, tool design is optimized using CAD software. Our engineering team, with years of experience, ensures optimal tool geometry, cutting edge strength, and chip evacuation performance.

03

Precision Machining & Forming

Using high-precision equipment such as 5-axis CNC machining centers, EDM, and laser cutting to ensure tool geometric accuracy down to micron level. The tool body is made of high-quality carbide or high-speed steel, heat-treated to enhance strength and toughness.

04

Welding & Brazing

Using vacuum brazing or high-frequency welding technology to firmly bond the PCD/PCBN insert to the tool body. Strict control of welding temperature and time ensures high bond strength, minimal heat-affected zone, and avoids insert damage.

05

Grinding & Polishing

Using imported high-precision CNC grinding machines with diamond wheels for edge finishing. Cutting edge roughness Ra ≤ 0.2μm ensures sharp cutting and smooth chip flow. Finally, surface polishing and anti-rust treatment are applied.

06

Quality Inspection & Packaging

Each tool undergoes comprehensive inspection with high-end equipment. Inspection items include dimensional accuracy, geometric angles, and cutting edge quality. Qualified products are packed in special packaging to avoid transportation damage.
