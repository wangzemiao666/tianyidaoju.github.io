About Tianyi Tools

Dalian Tianyi Cutting Tools Co., Ltd. is located in the coastal city of Dalian, surrounded by mountains and sea with convenient transportation. We specialize in the field of superhard tools, producing diamond (PCD) and cubic boron nitride (PCBN) tools.

Since our establishment in 2009, we have developed a stable production scale. Our products are ISO9001 certified and have earned customer recognition for their reliable quality.

"Quality first, never satisfied" is our eternal pursuit. We sincerely invite elites from all walks of life to join hands and draw a new blueprint for industry development.
